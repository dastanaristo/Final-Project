How to improve your house efficiency:

Consider the following tips to improve your house electricity efficiency:

  • Use energy-efficient appliances.
  • Install LED light bulbs.
  • Upgrade insulation to reduce heating and cooling energy consumption.
  • Turn off lights and appliances when not in use.
  • Use programmable thermostats to optimize heating and cooling schedules.
